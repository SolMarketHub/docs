## What is SolMarket?

SolMarket is a **parimutuel prediction market** built on Solana where users bet on whether a memecoin's price will go **Up** or **Down** within a given timeframe.

All funds are held by a **smart contract** — never by the team. Payouts are automatic, proportional, and verifiable on-chain.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Non-custodial" icon="shield-halved">
    Funds are held in Program Derived Addresses (PDAs) on Solana. No human has the private key.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Parimutuel" icon="scale-balanced">
    The entire pool is split among winners proportionally to their shares. Simple, fair, transparent.
  </Card>
  <Card title="On-chain" icon="link">
    Every bet, every payout, every resolution is a Solana transaction. Fully verifiable by anyone.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works in 30 seconds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a market">
    Choose a memecoin prediction — e.g. *"Will $BONK be above $0.00003 in 1 hour?"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take a side">
    Buy **Up** or **Down** shares with USDC. Each share costs between 1¢ and 99¢ based on current probability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for resolution">
    The oracle checks the token price at expiry via DexScreener's public API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get paid">
    If you're right, claim your proportional share of the entire pool. If nobody resolves, you get a refund.
  </Step>
</Steps>
