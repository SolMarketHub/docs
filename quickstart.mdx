## Prerequisites

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Solana Wallet" icon="wallet">
    Install [Phantom](https://phantom.app) or [Solflare](https://solflare.com) browser extension.
  </Card>
  <Card title="USDC on Solana" icon="dollar-sign">
    You need USDC (SPL token) on Solana. You can buy it on any exchange and withdraw to your wallet.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Step-by-step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Go to [solmarket.app](https://solmarket.app) and click **Connect Wallet** in the top right. Select Phantom or Solflare.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse markets">
    The home page shows all active prediction markets. Each card shows:
    - The memecoin being tracked (e.g. $BONK, $WIF)
    - The target price and direction
    - Time remaining until resolution
    - Current probability (Up vs Down)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Buy shares">
    Click on a market, then:
    1. Choose **Up** (you think the price will be above the target) or **Down** (below)
    2. Enter the USDC amount you want to bet
    3. Review the number of shares and cost
    4. Click **Buy** and confirm in your wallet

    <Tip>
      Share prices range from 1¢ to 99¢. The cheaper the shares, the higher the potential payout — but the less likely the market thinks that outcome is.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for resolution">
    When the market reaches its expiry time, the oracle automatically checks the token price and resolves the market.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Claim winnings or refund">
    - **If you win**: Click **Claim** on the market page. Your proportional share of the pool is sent to your wallet.
    - **If you lose**: No action needed. Your shares have no remaining value.
    - **If not resolved**: After the deadline passes, you can claim a **full refund**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key concepts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is parimutuel betting?">
    All bets go into a shared pool. When the market resolves, the **entire pool** is split among winners proportionally to the number of shares they hold. There is no counterparty — you're betting against the pool.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are share prices determined?">
    Share prices reflect the market's current probability. If 70% of the money is on Up, an Up share costs ~70¢ and a Down share costs ~30¢. Prices naturally move as people bet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if nobody resolves the market?">
    The smart contract has a built-in refund mechanism. If the market isn't resolved within the deadline, any participant can trigger a refund to get their USDC back.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a fee?">
    A small protocol fee (1%) is taken from winning claims. This fee funds protocol development and $SOLMARKET buybacks. See [Fee Structure](/protocol/fees) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
