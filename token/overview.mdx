## Overview

**$SOLMARKET** is the native token powering the SolMarket prediction market protocol. It provides utility within the ecosystem and benefits from a deflationary buyback & burn mechanism funded directly by protocol revenue.

<Info>
  **Status**: Pre-launch. Token details below are confirmed for the upcoming launch.
</Info>

## Token utility

### Current
- **Governance signal** — $SOLMARKET holders can participate in community votes for new market types, fee changes, and protocol upgrades
- **Deflationary asset** — Continuous buyback & burn creates value accrual for holders
- **Community alignment** — Holding $SOLMARKET means being invested in the protocol's success

### Planned (Roadmap)
- **Fee discounts** — Reduced protocol fees for users staking $SOLMARKET
- **Priority market creation** — Token holders can propose and vote on new markets
- **Revenue sharing** — Staking $SOLMARKET to earn a share of protocol fees

## Launch timeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pre-launch (Current)">
    Documentation, community building, and protocol testing on devnet. No token exists yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Token launch on Meteora DBC">
    $SOLMARKET launches on Meteora's Dynamic Bonding Curve with initial fee structure of **2.5%** trading fee.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Migration to DAMM v2">
    At **$60,000 market cap**, the token automatically migrates from DBC to Meteora's DAMM v2 pool with a reduced **1.5%** trading fee.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Full ecosystem activation">
    Buyback & burn mechanism activates. Protocol fees start flowing into token buybacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
